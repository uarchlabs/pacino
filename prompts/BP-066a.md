## Background
The confidence mechanism was redefined (session-053). conf is now a
bimodal DIRECTION counter: its MSB is FTB's predicted direction. The
always_taken bit is removed (conf is the sole direction state). The
threshold-suppression policy is replaced by a fast-path: when
ftb_fastpath_en=1 and conf is saturated (all-ones or all-zeros), FTB
commits its direction at s2 and the cluster does not apply a TAGE/SC
direction override for that branch. Authority: ftb_decisions.md
(4.3, 5.4, 5.5, 7, 8), ftb_confidence_override_rules.md (full), and
ftb_interfaces.md (2.3, 2.4, 2.5, IC-FTB-06/08).

This task edits ftb_cntrl.sv (BP-066), the ftb top (BP-067), and the
FTB confidence parameters in bp_defines_pkg.sv. ftb_array.sv and
ftb_plru.sv are parameter-driven and are NOT edited -- their data
widths follow FTB_RAM_ENTRY_WIDTH / FTB_RAM_SET_WIDTH from the package.
