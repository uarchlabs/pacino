## Background
ftb (the structural top over ftb_array data RAM, ftb_plru valid+PLRU,
ftb_cntrl logic) is complete (BP-065..067, BP-066a/b). This task
verifies it through its external contract only. Behavior authority:
ftb_decisions.md (1, 4, 4.2, 4.5, 5.1-5.5, 7, 8.1),
ftb_confidence_override_rules.md (sections 2-9, the conf direction +
fast-path), and ftb_interfaces.md (sections 2, 4 invariants).

conf is a BIMODAL DIRECTION counter: ftb_brI_taken_p2 = valid &
conf[MSB]. The fast-path output is ftb_fastpath_p2 (gated by the input
ftb_fastpath_en). There is no always_taken bit and no ftb_upd_ftb_dir_u0
input. Position is sourced from ftb_upd_pos_u0 and read on
ftb_br0_pos_p2 / ftb_br1_pos_p2 / ftb_jmp_pos_p2 (BP-066b).

The DUT exposes one update port and one read port; drive them in
SEPARATE cycles (timing section below). There is no FTQ -- do not build
one.

## Binding Previous Decisions
- DUT is the ftb top. Instantiate ftb and drive only its top ports. Do
  not bind into or drive ftb_array / ftb_plru / ftb_cntrl internals --
  testing through the top keeps the array-substitution invariant honest.
- Timing / read-port sharing (BP-066, IC-FTB-09). Prediction is two
  cycles: drive pred_valid_p0 / pred_pc_p0 at cycle N, sample the p2
  outputs at cycle N+2. An active update borrows the single read port,
  so a prediction issued in the same cycle as an update self-bubbles
  (its p2 outputs read invalid). DRIVE PREDICTION AND UPDATE IN SEPARATE
  CYCLES; do not issue both in one cycle and do not treat the bubbled
  prediction as a failure. After an update writes at cycle N, the new
  entry state is visible to a prediction issued at cycle >= N+1.
- conf is bimodal direction. Drive ftb_upd_taken_u0 (resolved outcome)
  directly; there is no ftb_upd_ftb_dir_u0. Direction under test is the
  conf MSB on ftb_brI_taken_p2.
- Tag/index are direct PC slices, not a hash. Derive index/tag from the
  parameters; do not assume. For the tree-PLRU eviction test, establish
  the target set's four ways from a known reset + driven allocate
  sequence; do not rely on residue; account for any aliasing way.
- Do not peek raw ftb_array contents for an unallocated entry (it is
  uninitialized / valid-gated); read only through the top, qualified by
  ftb_valid_p2 and the field valids.
